Kimchi Jjigae

Kimchi Jjigae

    Ingredients:

  • kimchi sliced into small pieces
  • kimchi brine
  • pound pork pork belly
  • optional cube-cut tofu
  • green onions
  • sliced onion
  • salt
  • sugar
  • Korean hot pepper flakes
  • Korean hot pepper paste
  • sesame oil
  • chicken broth

To make kimchee Jjigae bring water to a boil. Add the kimchee and chicken broth. Allow to boil for about 10 minutes. Add in the pork belly and allow to boil for another 5 minutes. Add all of the vegetables and let cook for 3 minutes add salt, spices, oil and tofu. Let it cool before you eat it! Fun fact: Koreans usually eat their soups while the soups are still boiling.
