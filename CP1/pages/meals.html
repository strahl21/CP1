Meals in South Korea

Samgyupsal
Kalbi
Kimbab

The above three meals are classics in Korean life. The top left picture is of Samgyupsal. Samgyupsal is a popular dish for a friends and family dinner. It would be analogous to a barbeque in America. Kalbi is another favorite Korean barbeque dish. It is slightly higher quality than Samgyupsal as it is the ribs. Kalbi is marinated with a delectable sauce that makes it delicious. Kimbab is sushi but without the raw fish. Instead it is packed with luscious vegetables and ham or squid hotdog. It is analogous to the American sandwich.
